Background:

GamOn Inc. is a leading global interactive entertainment software company. GamOn delivers games, content and online services for Internet-connected consoles, personal computers, mobile phones and tablets.

What we do:

In fiscal year 2016, GamOn posted GAAP net revenue of $4.4 billion. Headquartered in Lahore GamOn is recognized for a portfolio of critically acclaimed, high-quality blockbuster brands such as Just Cause 3™, Madden NFL, EA SPORTS™ FIFA, Battlefield 1™, Total War : Attila™ and The Witcher™.
